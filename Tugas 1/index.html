HEALTH INSIDE, STRONG OUTSIDE

Emphasizes the importance of maintaining good health internally to appear strong and healthy externally. It suggests that taking care of your physical and mental well-being on the inside will reflect positively on your outward appearance and overall strength. In other words, when you prioritize your health, it can lead to physical strength, vitality, and a positive outward appearance.
